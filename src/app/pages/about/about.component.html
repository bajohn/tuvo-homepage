About

Tuvo LLC has consulted for the National Institutes of Health (NIH) Central Utility Plant in Bethesda, Maryland for several years, providing software solutions tailored to the campus's needs.
Sample applications include:
  • Daily data reporting tools for upper management.
  • Mobile-friendly data entry forms for operators.
  • Interactive visualization websites for engineers and data scientists.
This work has been presented in several international conferences, including:
  • CampusEnergy2019 Conference
  • OSISoft PI World 2018, where NIH won an award for Digital Sustainability Improvements.
  • OSISoft PI World 2017
